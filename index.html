How to Play

  • 🏰 You stand on top of the castle.
  • ⬅️➡️ Move with the arrows / A D keys or the on-screen buttons.
  • ⬆️ Jump (Space / ↑ / JUMP button) to touch a dragon — it turns into a star!
  • 🔥 Don't get hit by falling fire — you lose a ❤️.
  • ⭐ Catch dragons quickly in a row for combo bonus points.
  • 🗺️ Adventure: clear 12 levels. ♾️ Endless: survive as long as you can!
  • 👗 Collect dragons and score points to unlock new costumes.
